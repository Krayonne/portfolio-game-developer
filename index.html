About Me

Who I am, what I do, and why I make games

Thanadul Suanma | Unity Developer

I'm a 3rd-year Game Design student at SPU who got into Unity and C# early and stuck with it. I like understanding how games work — the systems behind the mechanics, the code that makes a fight feel responsive or a frame rate stay stable.

Most of my work is on the programming side: gameplay systems, multiplayer netcode, and getting performance where it needs to be. If something's laggy or jank, I want to fix it. That's just who I am.

Outside of pure code, I use tools like Antigravity, Gemini CLI, and Claude to work faster — not to skip thinking, just to spend more time on actual problems.
