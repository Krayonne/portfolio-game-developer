# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Technical recruiters, game studio hiring managers, and potential collaborators.

## Product Purpose
A personal portfolio website for Thanadul Suanma to showcase Unity game development skills, 3D modeling, and audio work, primarily for internship applications.

## Brand Personality
Technical, Innovative, Professional.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS templates (feels unoriginal)
- Hard-to-read interfaces (low contrast, cluttered)
- "Ugly" or unpolished designs (lacking attention to detail)
- Non-unique/boilerplate layouts

## Design Principles
- **Technical Excellence:** Show off the "mechanical" side—performance, systems, and optimization—through the interface itself.
- **Innovative Interaction:** Use subtle, unique interactions that reflect a "game developer" mindset.
- **Professional Polish:** Every alignment, transition, and color choice must feel intentional and high-quality.
- **Clarity First:** Innovation should never come at the cost of readability or finding information quickly.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Target WCAG AA compliance for contrast and readability.
- Support for reduced motion (e.g., the petal animation).
